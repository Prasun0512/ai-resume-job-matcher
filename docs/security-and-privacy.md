# Security and Privacy

This document complements `docs/security-and-governance.md`.

## Candidate Privacy

- Do not commit real resumes, private job descriptions, contact details, or recruiter notes.
- Redact emails, phone numbers, addresses, and identifiers in examples.
- Keep sample data synthetic and interview-safe.

## Responsible AI

- The matcher is not a hiring decision engine.
- Do not infer protected characteristics.
- Show evidence, gaps, and uncertainty clearly.
- Require human review for career or recruiting decisions.

## Production Controls

- Encrypt stored documents.
- Apply access control and retention policies.
- Log only sanitized summaries.
- Version scoring rules and synonym maps.
